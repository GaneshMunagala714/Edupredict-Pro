AI Degree Program Planning Tool

Data-driven decision intelligence for College Deans. Forecast enrollment, calculate ROI, and get honest recommendations for launching AI degree programs.

🎯 What Makes This Different

Unlike other forecasting tools, EduPredict admits when it's uncertain. It shows prediction ranges, flags low-confidence scenarios, and tells you "DO NOT LAUNCH" when the data suggests poor viability.

Key Features

📊

3-Year Enrollment Forecasting

Project student enrollment with prediction intervals. Get Year 1, 2, 3 forecasts with confidence scores (e.g., "40 students, range: 15-65, 65% confidence").

💰

ROI Analysis with Risk Flags

Calculate tuition revenue vs. program costs. The model flags financial risks and tells you when ROI is too low to justify launch.

💼

Workforce Intelligence

State-level AI job market data from BLS. Compare CT, NY, MA for job growth, salaries, and employer demand.

🎯

Honest Recommendations

STRONG GO / GO / CONDITIONAL / RECONSIDER / DO NOT LAUNCH. The model admits uncertainty instead of pretending confidence.

📄

Professional PDF Reports

Download executive summaries with key metrics, financial analysis, and recommendations for presentations.

⚠️

Uncertainty Quantification

See warning flags explaining why predictions are risky. Understand confidence levels before making decisions.
